About FALAHX

FALAHX is a next-gen BEP-20 token designed to disrupt traditional finance through automated, secure, and community-driven protocols. Built on the BNB Chain, FALAHX introduces smart transaction features like cooldown timers, anti-whale rules, auto-burn mechanisms, and low-tax incentives to maximize long-term growth while minimizing volatility.

Tokenomics

Total Supply: 10,000,000 FALAHX

Decimals: 18

Buy Tax: 0.5%

Sell Tax: 1%

Auto Burn: 0.2% of every transaction

Cooldown Time: 60 seconds

Max Wallet Limit: 2%

Max Transaction Limit: 1%

Features

  • Anti-Whale: Limits on wallet and transaction size prevent market manipulation.
  • Auto-Burn: 0.2% of each transaction is permanently burned, reducing supply.
  • Cooldown Timer: 60-second delay to prevent rapid trades and bots.
  • Adjustable Tax: Owner can adjust taxes to align with project needs.
  • Bot Protection: Logic to restrict suspicious contracts and behavior.
  • PinkLock Ready: Liquidity will be locked to build trust.

Smart Features to Protect Investors and Promote Fairness

  • Buy and Sell Taxes: Small fees (0.5% buy / 1% sell) fund development and marketing while encouraging long-term holding.
  • Auto-Burn: 0.2% of every transaction is burned to reduce supply and increase scarcity.

  • Editable Taxes with Limits: Ensures flexibility while maintaining fairness and community trust.
